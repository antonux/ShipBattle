# Shipbattle

A tactical naval strategy game built with **React** and **Firebase**.

---

## Objective

Destroy all enemy vessels before your opponent sinks yours. Victory depends on positioning, timing, and precision.

---

## How to Play

**01 — Join a Match**  
Enter a **room code** to create or join a game session.  
Choose your role: **Player 1** or **Player 2**.

**02 — Deploy Your Fleet**  
Place **five (5) vessels** on the grid.  
Press **`R`** to rotate ships before confirming placement.

**03 — Maintain Offensive Pressure**  
Each successful hit grants **one additional turn**.  
Use this advantage to chain attacks and control the battlefield.

**04 — Avoid Wasted Fire**  
A missed shot immediately **ends your turn**.  
Plan carefully—every move has consequences.

---

## Technology Stack

- **React** — Frontend and UI logic  
- **Firebase** — Backend and real-time game sync
